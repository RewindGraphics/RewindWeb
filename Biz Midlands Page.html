My Creative Journey At Biz Midlands.

Biz Midlands .

Biz Midlands Graphics

During my time at Biz Midlands, I had the opportunity to take full creative control over a variety of visual projects — from digital posters and social media graphics to animations and short promotional videos. I designed 20+ custom posters daily, each tailored to a different client’s brand identity, product range, and audience. These designs helped boost online engagement and created a consistent, professional look across multiple marketing platforms. I worked closely with business owners to understand their goals and transformed their ideas into eye-catching visuals that stood out both online and in print. As part of expanding our marketing strategy, I began creating animated content and short videos, experimenting with tools like CapCut and Adobe Premiere Pro. These animations were designed for social media campaigns, product launches, and seasonal promotions, allowing our clients to connect with audiences in more dynamic and engaging ways. Alongside this, I explored different design styles — from bold, modern layouts to minimalist, product-focused designs — to match each brand’s tone and message. I combined photography, typography, and motion graphics to tell each client’s story visually and effectively. My portfolio from this period includes: A range of digital posters and banners used across social media and websites Animated video ads promoting new product launches Photo-enhanced designs for product campaigns Experimental motion graphics projects exploring new trends in digital marketing This experience pushed me to develop my own creative workflow — balancing speed, consistency, and originality — while keeping each client’s identity at the heart of every design.
